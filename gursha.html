Delivery & Pricing

Once we have your order, we'll contact the restaurant and send a driver. We aim to deliver in an hour or less, but please be patient during peak hours. Deliveries are priced based on your distance from the restaurant. Once you're logged in and have saved your location, you'll be able to see which restaurants are available to order from.

How It Works

Select a Restaurant

Once you login and set your location, you'll be able to see all the restaurants within your delivery range. Pick a restaurant and see their menu.

Browse Their Menu

Once you've selected the restaurant you'd like to order from, take a look at their menu to figure out what you'd like to eat. Place your choices in your basket, tell us how many you'd like to order, and place your order!

Place Your Order

Once you have all your choices in your cart, click on "Place your order" and you'll receive an email or SMS confirmation. Sit back and relax as we work to get your order delivered to your door in an hour or less!
